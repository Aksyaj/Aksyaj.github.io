What effects does anxiety have on your brain

Anxiety stimulates your mind meaning that it makes more active and aware of its surroundings. Unlike a normal person someone with anxiety will constantly be worrying. Being anxious becomes part of their daily routine because that person can't control it. Its like breathing, most of the time it just happens and you can't suffocate yourself by holding ur breath as your brain automatically makes you gasp for air. Its the same with anxiety, their brains automatically get worried and they have little to no control over it.






What happens when you stress or have a panic attack?

Whenever you are stressed your brain releases stress hormones and adrenaline (epinephrine). This is a normal human reaction to tough situations as it gets you ready for something. However when these hormones are released in a large amount repeatively than thats when its called anxiety. Panic attacks are caused by the same hormones but they can be more severe than just getting nervous or scared.
