What NOT to do if you have Anxiety

1. Drink, even if alcohol relieves the stress, it is unhealthy and is extremely addictive.

2. Smoke, smoking like alcohol helps with short term relief but in the long race its addictive and it worsens the anxiety.

3. Have high caffeine drinks, caffeine is a drug that can worsen anxiety, those suffering from chronic anxiety would feel nervous and overthink. Those with panic disorders can have panic attacks after drinking something like coffee. Research has shown that eliminating caffeine drinks from your diet can help with anxiety.








How to relieve Anxietic stress

1. Meditate

2. Don't use your look at any screens within an hour of your bedtime

3. Don't eat way too much before sleeping

4. Have a sleep scedule

5. Eat healthy

6. Stay active, exercises such as yoga help take your mind off the stress
